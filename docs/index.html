Bagelsaurus

Bagelsaurus is the greatest restaurant in Cambridge, Massachusetts. In addition to having delicious bagels and sandwiches, here are a few other reasons why Bagelsaurus is the best:

  1. It has the greatest logo of all time.
  2. The store was funded via Kickstarter in 2013.
  3. Their website will instantly make you hungry.
  4. A grab bag of bagels & cream cheese is an amazing breakfast for the week.
  5. They sell sweatshirts with the dinosaur logo.

What to eat

It's very hard to go wrong, but it's easy to feel overwhelmed when confronted with such a delicious menu in a very slow-moving line. Here's some guidance:

Menu Item Guidance
Classic Jumbo: Egg, Cheddar, Mustard Butter, Avocado This sandwich is always delicious. Try it on a chedder-garlic bagel.
Bagel with Honey Rosemary Cream Cheese This cream cheese is amazing, especially on a sesame or pretzel bagel.
T-Rex: Almond Butter, Banana, Honey, Bacon I would like someone else to order this and tell me how it is.
Bagelsaurus coffee Could be a lot better. Go next door to Simon's if you have time.
Bagel with beet hummus I've only seen one person order this, and the person at the register looked upset.

When to go

Bagelsaurus gets very crowded at certain times, so set your expectations based on when you go:

'No line' times: Anytime besides 12pm on weekdays or before 9am on weekends (this is the best time, because of the weekend cheddar-garlic bagels)

'Short line' times: Between 9-9:45am or after 2pm on weekends.

'Wait in line' times: Bring a friend, book, charged phone, and small snack if you are going between 9:45 - 2pm on a weekend, as you'll be in line for 40-60 minutes. Also bring sunscreen or an umbrella depending on the weather, since you'll be outside for the majority of your wait.

Bagelsaurus alternatives

If you've moved to Chicago, you are going to be OK because you can go to Chicago Bagel Authority. The bagels are different, but still delicious.
